# Delft3d
A repos for deft3d files.

To download GEBCO depth NC file, please open the following link, choose your coordinates in lon/lat format and download the select the NC option 
https://download.gebco.net/

To download wind data, please open the following link and first register, go to the download tab, tick off the reanalysis box and in the variables section, tick off 10m u-component of wind and 10m v-component of wind then download u10 wind, then scroll down and choose year, month,day and hour of data and finally at the Geographical area, write your extent and remember the wind extent must be larger than the delft3d computtional grid so delft3d can interpolte wind on its own grid.

https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/datasets/reanalysis-era5-single-levels?tab=download
