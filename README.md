# Delft3d
A repos for deft3d files.

To download GEBCO depth NC file, please open the following link, click on select subset options, choose your coordinates in lon/lat format, click on layers and formats, choose bathymetry from Layers and NC from Formats. Hit the basket icon from the top right, write your E-mail address and wait for an E-mail from the website.

https://download.gebco.net/

To download wind data, please open the following link and first register, then go to the download tab, tick off the reanalysis box and in the variables section, tick off 10m u-component of wind and 10m v-component of wind, then scroll down and choose year, month, day and hour of data and finally at the Geographical area, write your extent and remember the wind extent must be larger than the delft3d computational grid so delft3d can interpolate wind on its own grids.

https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/datasets/reanalysis-era5-single-levels?tab=download
